About Us

Is your student struggling with test anxiety, inconsistent scores, or not sure where to start? Topscore Method is a clear, strategic path designed to boost confidence, performance, and real SAT and ACT results in math and science. At Topscore Method, students have the potential to excel since they are given the right tools, the right strategy, and the right support. Developed by expert tutor Judy DaCosta, PhD, the proprietary method follows both the SAT and ACT tests closely, diagnoses student misunderstandings and weaknesses, and rebuilds confidence through targeted practice, custom hints and strategies, and refined repetition. This is NOT tutoring off the shelf. This is tutoring that works because it is built around why students struggle, and how they succeed. It is an individualized program with mindset coaching to unlock each student’s full potential. The result? A smarter way to prepare, and scores that reflect it.

Pillars to Peak Performance

Precision Diagnosis

A targeted diagnostic test is given to identify strengths, pinpoint weak spots, and uncover hidden gaps. This ensures no time is wasted and every personalized session is focused where it matters most.

Skill-Specific Training

Using Topscore Method proprietary practice sets tailored to each student’s needs, complex topics are broken down into simple, teachable steps. Topscore Method builds fluency, speed, and accuracy through strategic, focused repetition.

Peak Test Mindset

Mastery isn’t just about knowing the material. It’s about owning the test! Topscore Method students are coached on managing anxiety, building test-day confidence, and developing a calm, focused mindset under pressure.
