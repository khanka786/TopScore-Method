PSAT Prep with the TOPSCORE Method

Laying the foundation for future success—strategically and confidently.

The PSAT isn’t just a practice SAT—it's the first major step in a student’s college admissions journey. Strong PSAT scores can lead to National Merit recognition and valuable scholarship opportunities, as well as build confidence for the SAT or ACT.

At TOPSCORE Method, we approach PSAT preparation with the same precision and strategy we bring to SAT and ACT prep. Students receive:

  • A targeted diagnostic to identify strengths and gaps early
  • Proprietary skill-building materials tailored to their needs
  • Test-day mindset coaching to build confidence from the start
  • Guidance on PSAT vs. SAT vs. ACT, including percent probability of question types and optimal timing

Our PSAT program doesn’t just prepare students for a single test—it sets the tone for high performance across their entire college admissions timeline.
