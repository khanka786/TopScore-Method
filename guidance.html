PSAT Prep with the TOPSCORE Method

Laying the foundation for future success—strategically and confidently.

The PSAT isn’t just a practice SAT—it's the first major step in a student’s college admissions journey. Strong PSAT scores can lead to National Merit recognition and valuable scholarship opportunities, as well as build confidence for the SAT or ACT.

At TOPSCORE Method, we approach PSAT preparation with the same precision and strategy we bring to SAT and ACT prep. Students receive:

  • A targeted diagnostic to identify strengths and gaps early
  • Proprietary skill-building materials tailored to their needs
  • Test-day mindset coaching to build confidence from the start
  • Guidance on PSAT vs. SAT vs. ACT, including percent probability of question types and optimal timing

Our PSAT program doesn’t just prepare students for a single test—it sets the tone for high performance across their entire college admissions timeline.

Student Responsibility

TOPSCORE Method delivers results—but success requires commitment.

TOPSCORE is a proven, high-performance system—but it is not magic. Meaningful score increases come from a true partnership between tutor and student. To get the most out of this program, students must:

  • Complete all assigned homework thoroughly and on time, using the strategies and notes provided during sessions.
  • Engage actively during tutoring sessions, asking questions and taking ownership of their learning.
  • Take the process seriously, treating preparation with the same level of importance as a high-stakes academic course.
  • Review mistakes thoughtfully—not just for correctness, but to understand the why behind each error.
  • Practice consistently outside of sessions to reinforce skills and build the stamina needed for high-stakes testing.

Our most successful students are those who show up prepared, put in the work, and trust the process. When they do, the results speak for themselves.
